A Simple 3-Step Guide to Selling Crypto on Coinbase

Step 1: Set Up Your Coinbase Account

The first step to selling crypto in Singapore is to create an account on Coinbase. Here’s how:

  1. Visit the Coinbase Website: Go to Coinbase and click on the “Get Started” button.

  2. Sign Up: Enter your email address and create a secure password. Make sure to use a strong password to enhance the security of your account.

  3. Verify Your Identity: As part of compliance with regulations, Coinbase requires users to verify their identity. This involves uploading a government-issued ID and a selfie. Verification typically takes a few minutes to a couple of hours.

Step 2: Transfer Crypto to Coinbase

Once your account is set up, the next step is to transfer your crypto assets to your Coinbase wallet:

  1. Navigate to Your Wallet: Log in to your Coinbase account and access your wallet.

  2. Select the Cryptocurrency: Choose the specific cryptocurrency you wish to sell.

  3. Copy Your Wallet Address: Each cryptocurrency has a unique wallet address. Copy the address and use it to transfer your crypto from your external wallet to Coinbase.

  4. Confirm the Transfer: Double-check the wallet address to ensure accuracy before confirming the transfer. Once confirmed, it may take a few minutes for the crypto to appear in your Coinbase wallet.

Step 3: Sell Your Cryptocurrency

With your crypto now in Coinbase, you’re ready to sell:

  1. Go to the Trade Section: Click on “Trade” and then select “Sell”.

  2. Choose the Crypto and Amount: Select the cryptocurrency you want to sell and enter the amount.

  3. Select Payout Method: Decide how you want to receive your funds. Coinbase allows direct bank transfers for Singaporean users, offering a secure and quick way to access your money.

  4. Confirm the Sale: Review the transaction details, including any fees, and confirm the sale. The funds will be transferred to your chosen payout method once the transaction is complete.
