Legal Considerations

Before diving into the process of buying Ethereum, it's important to note the regulatory environment in India. While cryptocurrencies are not illegal, they are not classified as legal tender. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has issued guidelines for businesses and individuals engaging in cryptocurrency activities. Always ensure compliance with local regulations and consider consulting a financial advisor if you have any doubts.
